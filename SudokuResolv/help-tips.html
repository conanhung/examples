In order to improve accuracy of the OCR functionality, there are a number of things you can do taking the picture of the puzzle:

  • Take the picture upright, fill as much of the screen as possible with content of the puzzle. Do crop the input image when necessary;
  • Maintain even lighting on surface of the sheet, and avoid sharp shadow;
  • Unwrinkle the sheet and clean up spots, as noises significantly reduce accuracy;
  • Do use the Numpad to correct wrong OCR guesses in Preview screen;
  • Don't try weird input cases, because you probably will win;
  • Enjoy.
